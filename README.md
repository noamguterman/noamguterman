💡 Frontend Developer | Creative Technologist
---------------------------------------------

✏️ I'm a frontend developer with a creative tech background. With a Master of Arts in Sound Design and a decade of experience in interactive media, AR/VR, and game development, I bring a unique approach to building dynamic, high-performance web applications. I specialize in React, JavaScript, and AI-powered applications, focusing on responsive design, performance optimization, and seamless user experiences. Over the past year, I’ve built projects that blend technical precision with creative problem-solving, always striving for clean, accessible, and engaging web solutions. When I’m not coding, I’m geeking out over new tech, solving puzzles, or rocking out to Dream Theater. Let’s build something awesome together! 🚀
